Directions To Make A NY Style Pizza

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and rease a 14-inch pizza pan. .
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Shape the crust; pinching the edge to form a narrow rim.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce over the crust, spooning it over the surface and using.
  5. Add Mozzarella and Romano cheeses, basil, oregano and crushed red pepper flakes.
  6. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  7. Bake on a lower oven rack for 15 to 20 minutes, until the crust is lightly browned and the cheese is melted.
  8. Rest! Let your pizza rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
