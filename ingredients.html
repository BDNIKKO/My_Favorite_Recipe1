Ingredients To Make NY Style Pizza

  • Pizza dough - 1 ball
  • Flour - for dusting
  • Tomato sauce - 1 can (8 oz)
  • Mozzarella cheese - 1 cup, shredded
  • Romano cheese 1/2 cup, grated
  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: Basil, fresh chopped
